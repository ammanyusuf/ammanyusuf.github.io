I'm currently part of Dr. Mijung Park's lab on privacy and safety as an MSc student

Current interests: diffusion models, text diffusion models, privacy, safety, and responsible AI.

I attended the CIFAR Deep Learning + Reinforcement Learning (DLRL) Summer School hosted by Amii, where I
focused on reinforcement learning, heard talks from Adam White, Marlos Machado, Martha White, and Richard
Sutton, explored KAN networks in RL settings, and made strong connections across the international deep
learning community.

Previously, I worked under the supervision of Dr. Tamer Jarada (internship - machine learning for healthcare
data) and Dr. Farhad Maleki (bachelor's honours thesis - federated learning and computer vision).

I also worked at [EV.com](https://ev.com/) under the supervision of Haoxu Wang, where I refined my software development skills
and started working on [CarAI](https://www.carai.com/) (a voice agent) using RAG, GraphRAG, LLMs, and voice
agent systems.
