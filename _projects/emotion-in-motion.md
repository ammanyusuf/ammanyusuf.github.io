Human-robot interaction study focused on expressive motion in small robots.

Highlights:
- Programmed Sony Toio robots in JavaScript to model participant-defined emotions.
- Analyzed motion patterns and proposed strategies for expressive non-anthropomorphic robots.

Tools: JavaScript, HRI, Sony Toio
